### Grammer
```text
grammer is the set of structural rules governing the composition of clauses,
phrases and words in a natural language.

example - 
  Here is a grammar, specified in a simple notation known as Backus-Naur Form or BNF [1]:

  S → 0 S 0

  S → 1 S 1

  S → 00

  S → 11
  
  The language which can be formed by this grammer are these and many more can be formed.
    00
    10111101
    110011
```
